What Is It

Ethan's Tank Game is a multiplayer tank game with 80's synthwave/retrowave styling, It is a solo indie developed project

with a large collection of interesting maps and gamemodes to explore and play

empty space

GameModes

Free For All

Free for all, is a gamemode with no teams, every tank is on its own battling for victory.
Capture The Flag

Capture the flag, 2 teams attempt to claim the others flag for points in the battle, with the team that captures the other teams flag the most being the winning team.

empty space

Capture The Power

Capture the power, (capture the points), where each team scores points based on the amount of power they have collected, the power points to be captured are positioned around the maps it is up to a team to capture and then defend their claimed points from other teams. The team also needs enough power for their dead players to respawn.
Team Death Match

Team DeathMatch, teams attempt to eliminate the other teams each round ,with 3 rounds each game the winning team is the one with the most rounds won over the other teams.

empty space

Respawn Wars

Respawn Wars, (inspired by bedwars/eggwars), is overall similar to Capture the power, however it is more difficult as power must manually be collected and returned to the respawner, in addition the tank shields do not work in the same way as they do in the rest of the game, in this gamemode the player gets to decide out of the power they have collected, how much they want to put into their own shields, or into the teams respawner.

Tank Customisation

The Tanks have a number of different configurations to choose from.

empty space

One of the ways this is accomplished is through different levels of shield.

empty space

Where no shield/or a low power shield will give the player the most speed and acceleration, but it will also take less damage to destroy the tank.

empty space

Where as a mid to high power shield will give the player the most shielding against damage at the expense of speed and acceleration.

empty space

(when in the Respawn Wars GameMode the shield works differently)


Another customisable aspect is the tank weapon.


Normal

A laser beam that upon hitting the target damages the tank.
Rapid

A faster reloading laser beam that operates the same as the Normal weapon but has slightly less damage, although this is offset by the faster reload times.

empty space

Fire

A wide spread damage weapon, that damages the enemy tanks, but only works at close ranges.
Freeze

A wide spread damage weapon, that not only damages the enemy tanks, but also slows them down for a temporary amount of time, at the cost of slightly reduced damage, only works at close ranges.
