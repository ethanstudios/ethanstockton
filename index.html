What Is It

Ethan's Tank Game is a multiplayer 80's styled tank game, solo indie developed project

with a currently expanding collection of interesting maps and gamemodes to explore and play



Tank Customisation



The tanks have different customisable aspects, allowing the player to make their tank play to their stengths

one of the ways this is acomplished is through different levels of shield

where no shield/or a low power shield will give the player the most speed it will also take less damage to destroy the tank

where as a mid to high power shield will give the player the most damage taking abiliy at the expence of speed and acceleration

(when in the Respawn Wars GameMode the shield works differently)


Another customisable aspect is the tank weapon


Normal

The Default Weapon
Rapid

The Default Weapon But With Faster Reload Time At The Cost Of Slightly Reduced Damage
Fire

A Flame Thrower
Freeze

A Freeze Thrower, Slightly Redused Damage Compared With The Flame Thrower But Has A Slowing Effect





GAMEMODES

Free For All

A free for all battle against other players with no teams
Capture The Flag

A battle between teams for capturing the most flags


Capture The Power

Capture the most power points in order to capture the most power for your team and win the game
Team Death Match

A round based battle to the death, with each team trying to knock the other teams out and finish the round as the winners


Respawn Wars

My take on the popular gamemodes of bedwars/eggwars, where each team is simoltaniously attempting to destroy the other teams respawner, as well as collect energy that can be used to respawn and improve there tanks shield
